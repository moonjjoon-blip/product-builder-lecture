About

What does this site provide?

This site is an interactive face-vibe test that estimates whether a face image reads closer to a puppy face or a cat face. Instead of showing only a label, it also explains how to interpret the output, what the limits are, and how to use the tool more effectively.

Privacy

Image handling and privacy notes

  • Webcam and uploaded-image analysis runs inside the user's browser session.
  • The site does not connect the chosen photo to an account profile or store it as a member asset.
  • Webcam access follows normal browser permissions and can be revoked by the user at any time.
  • The tool is not designed for identity verification or sensitive personal-data collection.

Editorial Policy

Content and quality standards

  • Prioritize clear explanations and a navigable page structure.
  • Prefer genuinely useful guides, FAQs, and policy notes over duplicated or thin pages.
  • Do not position advertising more prominently than the actual content experience.
  • State the entertainment-oriented limits of the AI tool and avoid exaggerated claims.
